## ingredients

* 100ml sunflower oil
* 200g self-raising flour
* 1 tsp ground cinnamon
* 1 tsp baking powder
* 200g caster sugar
* 40g desiccated coconut
* 2 eggs
* 1 over-ripe banana, mashed
* 100g carrots, grated
* 300g crushed pineapple, drained
* 60ml milk

## method

1. Heat oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4.
2. Grease and line the bases of 2 x 20cm sandwich tins with baking parchment.
3. Mix the flour, cinnamon, baking powder and the coconut in a large mixing bowl.
4. In another bowl mix the beaten whole eggs, mashed banana, grated carrot, drained crushed pineapple, sugar, milk and oil.
5. Stir the dry mixture into the wet until smooth and lump-free.
6. Divide evenly between the tins and bake for 25 mins until risen and a skewer comes out clean.
